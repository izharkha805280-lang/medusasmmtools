About Zakat

Zakat is one of the Five Pillars of Islam. It is a mandatory charitable contribution for all Muslims who meet the necessary criteria of wealth (Nisab). It is calculated as 2.5% of your total net assets held for one lunar year.

How to use:

  • Gold/Silver: Enter the current market value of your jewelry or bars.
  • Cash: Include money in savings, checking accounts, and cash at home.
  • Investments: Value of stocks, crypto, or business merchandise.
  • Liabilities: Enter any immediate debts you owe (this reduces your taxable amount).
