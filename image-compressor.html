About Image Compressor

This tool allows you to reduce the file size of your images (JPEG/PNG) without needing to upload them to a server. Everything happens on your device.

How to use:

  • Click "Choose File" and select an image.
  • Adjust the quality slider (Lower number = smaller size but lower quality).
  • Click "Compress Now".
  • Download your new lightweight image.
