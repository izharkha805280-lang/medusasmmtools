How Binary Works?

Computers don't understand English; they understand electricity (On/Off), which is represented by 1 and 0. This tool translates each character of your text into its 8-bit binary equivalent (ASCII).

Example:

  • Letter 'A' = 01000001
  • Letter 'B' = 01000010
