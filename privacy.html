Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

At MedusaSMM Tools, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users above all else. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we do not collect, how we handle user interactions, and our commitment to maintaining a secure digital environment. By using our website and its associated utilities, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collection Philosophy

We operate under a strict "Data Minimization" policy. Our tools are designed to perform functions without requiring personal identification. We explicitly state that:

  • No Account Required: You can access all our tools without creating an account, providing an email address, or setting up a password.
  • No Input Storage: Any text, numbers, or files you process through our tools are handled transiently. We do not store, archive, or review the data you input into our generators or converters.
  • No Personal Identifiable Information (PII): We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

2. Technical Log Files

Like most standard websites, MedusaSMM Tools utilizes log files for server health monitoring and security purposes. These logs may include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, and referring/exit pages. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information. It is used solely to analyze trends, administer the site, prevent DDoS attacks, and improve the overall user experience.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential cookies or local storage solely to enhance your browsing experience. For example, we may store a small file to remember your preference for "Dark Mode" or "Light Mode" so you don't have to reset it every time you visit. We do not use tracking cookies for third-party advertising or cross-site tracking purposes.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites or resources. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

5. Security Measures

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your visit. Our website is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. We regularly update our server infrastructure to protect against known vulnerabilities. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
