Why use a strong password?

Using weak passwords like "123456" or "password" makes you vulnerable to hackers. A strong password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

How to use this tool:

  • Drag the slider to choose your desired password length (8-64 characters).
  • The tool automatically creates a new random password.
  • Click "Regenerate Password" if you want a different one.
  • Copy the password and save it in a secure password manager.
