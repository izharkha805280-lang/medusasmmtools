About Age Calculator

This simple tool calculates your exact age based on the Gregorian calendar. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you a precise result.

How to use:

  • Tap on the date box.
  • Select your birth year, month, and day from the calendar.
  • Click the "Calculate" button.
  • See exactly how many Years, Months, and Days you have lived!
