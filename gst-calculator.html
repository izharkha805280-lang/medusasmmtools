About GST Calculator

This free tool helps you calculate Goods and Service Tax (GST) for Indian goods and services. You can easily find out the net price and gross price.

How to Use?

  • Enter the basic Amount.
  • Select the GST Slab (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%).
  • Click Add GST to add tax to the price.
  • Click Remove GST to subtract tax from the total price.
